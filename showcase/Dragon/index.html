ABOUT US

Dragon Eats & Lodge represents the pinnacle of modern Asian culinary artistry. Founded in 2015, our restaurant has become renowned for its innovative approach to traditional Japanese cuisine, particularly our exceptional sushi offerings.

Under the guidance of Master Chef Hiroshi Tanaka, our team of skilled artisans crafts each dish with precision and passion, using only the finest ingredients sourced daily from trusted local and international suppliers.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the kitchen, creating an atmosphere that honors both tradition and contemporary sophistication. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience that engages all the senses.
